ABOUT

Indian Society of Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country, with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System. It was established with a sudden urge for society which would focus on the promotion of the Fests, Sponsorships and towards enhancement of the practical, technical knowledge which the current education system lacks.


BVP ISTE is the student chapter of the Indian Society for Technical Education in a more hands-on approach. BVP ISTE has constantly strived to achieve its goal of focusing more on practical knowledge. We have tried to achieve this through various sessions, workshops and competitions in the field of hardware, software and research.

STUDENT INTEREST GROUPS

Electronika is the robotics SIG (Student Interest Group) of ISTE. It is a group that aims to teach students how they can learn, make and invent technology. It was started for the curious and the geek minds who love to experiment, have innovative ideas and are just finding an incentive to implement their ideas.

Softech is the coding SIG (Student Interest Group) of ISTE. It provides a great platform to learn, grow and showcase ane's technical skills and talent. It organizes various set workshops on different technical fields like machine learning, hacking, competitive coding etc. It was started for geeks who love being in the programming world and easily grabs the computer and its functions.

Sahitye is the literature SIG (Student Interest Group) of ISTE. It believes in giving the students a chance at exploring their creative side. It is a group that aims to ignite the interests of students which get lost under the heavy pressure of academic race. It was started for providing a platform for an introvert mind to express itself without any kind of fear or anxiety. It intends to develop confidence, both in front of a mass or in front of one's personal diary.
